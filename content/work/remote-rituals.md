I used to roll out of bed and open Slack. Now I treat the first hour as non-negotiable.

**What works for me:**

1. **No phone for 30 minutes.** Not even to check the weather. It can wait.
2. **Same spot, same drink.** Coffee at the same chair. Boring is the point.
3. **One hard thing before email.** Usually a block of writing or a focused coding task.

The rest of the day can be reactive. The first hour is for building.

You don’t need a fancy system. You need a default that’s good enough that you actually do it.
